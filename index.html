About Us

We are a thin crust pizza shop located at 767 Westminster Street in Providence, R.I. We serve thin and tasty pizza to all the skaters, students, hipsters, vegetarians, vegans, intellectuals, pseudo-intellectuals, and pretty much anyone who is into great food that is reasonably priced. We were started in 2005 by Al Read and Robert Murphy and have been doing it every day since. By “it” we mean opening at 11 am everyday to serve the best pizza you ever put your lips on. This is because we love pizza. It is the center of the universe and drives everything creative and wonderful. It brings people together and has mysterious power scientists have yet to fully comprehend...Come and have a slice to experience it for yourself.
