ABOUT OUR PIZZA

This is our classic NY Style, thin crust pizza. All pizzas are 18' and have 8 slices. These pizzas come with a blend of low moisture mozzarella and cheddar cheese, and tomato sauce unless specified. You can add toppings to our plain cheese or select from our list of specialty pizzas. Please note that many of our specialty pizzas do not have tomato sauce.
