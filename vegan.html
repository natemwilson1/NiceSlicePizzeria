ABOUT OUR VEGAN PIZZA

This is our VEGAN version of our classic NY Style, thin crust pizza. All pizzas are 18' and have 8 slices. These pizzas come with cheese substitute, and tomato sauce unless specified. You can add toppings to our plain vegan cheese or select from our list of specialty pizzas. Please note that many of our specialty pizzas do not have tomato sauce.
